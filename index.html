BUDIRIRO

Budiriro is a high density suburb in the southwestern area of Harare in Zimbabwe. There are about 30,000 houses but only two clinics and six primary schools and two high schools. In 2008, Budiriro had the highest number of cholera outbreaks. accounting for 50% of the reported cases in Zimbabwe.[3] Another common disease in Budiriro is typhoid. In late 2016, most of the suburb was affected by flash flooding.

ECONOMY

Vendors

A considerable amount of budiriro residents are vendors. They sell a range of products such as vegetables, fish, groceries, LP gas etc.

Transport

There is also a transport industry which is dominated by kombis, you can also find mushikashika although they are still a few of those Zupco is another player.

Shops

Budiriro has 6 official shopping centres namely Budiriro 1, Budiriro 2, Budiriro 3, Budiriro 4, Budiriro 5A and Budiriro 5B shopping centres. These shopping centres all have supermarkets, butcheries and mini malls.

Services

Service provision is one of the major contributors to the economy of the town. Some of these services include Barbershops, internet cafes, salons, printing shops etc

SCHOOLS

High Schools

Budiriro 1 High

The first high school to be built in the area, it opened in the early 2000s. it offers O level and A level. It is physically located in Budiriro 2.

Budiriro 2 High

The second high school to be built in Budiriro, it's physically located in Budiriro 5A area. It only offers O level for now.
